what is your favourite winter animal?

polar bear skin is actually black and their fur is translucent! they appear white because their fur reflects sunlight.

arctic fox dens are used by many generations. some are even over 300 years old!

reindeer are actually very good swimmers! they cross large rivers during migration and can swim 3 times the speed of the average human!

due to the cold and harsh climate, arctic hares survive with shortened ears and limbs, a small nose, a high body fat percentage, and a thick coat of fur.
